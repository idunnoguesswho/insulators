Procedure

  1. Step 1

    Draw a Plan View and letter as shown. Mark the true height on the right vertical line

  2. Step 2

    Trisect the bottom right quarter circle into equal parts and label

  3. Step 3

    Set dividers at Point A and Point 1 and swing an arc from the Base Line to the right vertical line. Do the same for Point 2. Points 1 and 4 intersect as do Points 2 and

  4. Step 4

    Set dividers at Point S and Point 4 and transfer to the horizontal line going from bottom right Point A to the left. Label the points on the horizontal line as shown

  5. Step 5

    Draw lines from the Apex (true height point) to all points on the horizontal line. These will be the slant heights

  6. Step 6

    For the Stretch-out, draw a line the exact length of a square side and label both Points A

  7. Step 7

    From the Plan View, set dividers at the Apex and Points 1- 4 on the horizontal line. Transfer these points to the layout from both Points A to an intersecting point on the Layout to form Point 1 on the Layout

  9. Step 9

  10. Step 10

    Do the same to obtain Points 3 and 4 on the Layout

  11. Step 11

    From Points A, draw arcs to either side using half the distance of a square side

  12. Step 12

    From the Plan View set dividers at the Apex and the S-4 Point and from the 4 Points on the Layout draw intersecting arcs to the last arcs drawn. These points will be Points S on the Layout

  13. Step 13
