Section 7: Short Radius Rubber 90 Degree Elbow

PreviousNext

Required Information

  • Pipe Call-out Size

Formulas and Measurements

  • Draw a 90 Degree angle Multiply the Pipe Call-out Size times Pi, divide by 2 and add 1 1/2 Swing an arc from the Apex.

Procedure

  1. Step 1

    Draw a 90 Degree angle

  2. Step 2

    Multiply the Pipe Call-out Size times Pi, divide by 2 and add 1 1/2

  3. Step 3

    Swing an arc from the Apex. Use this measurement to form Cut-line A

  4. Step 4

    Draw a 22 1/2 degree line from the Apex to Cut-line A from the horizontal line

  5. Step 5

    Add 1" to both the vertical and the 22 1/2 degree lines to form Cut-lines B & C

  6. Step 6

    Swing an arc for the Throat
