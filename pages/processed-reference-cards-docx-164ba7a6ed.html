2. Use the square to draw 90-degree lines through each mark, lines should be at least 1" longer than the 1/2 the heel measurement. See figure 2.
3. Line 1 is the throat line. Add 3/8" for the beat to the throat measurement. See figure 3.
4. Line #9 is your heel line. Add 3/4" for the crimp to your heel measurement and then divide by 2. Transfer the length to line #9. Example 6" Heel + 3/4" crimp = 6-3/4". Divide this by 2 to get 3-3/8". Transfer this to line 9.
