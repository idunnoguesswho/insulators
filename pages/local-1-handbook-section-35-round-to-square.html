35. Round to Square

Local 1 Handbook OCR · source PDF scan

Information required:

True height

Square dimensions

Round dimensions

Step 1. Draw an Elevation View of the fitting (include insulation).

Step 2. Draw a Plan View. Trisect the top right quarter circle and label as shown.

Step 3. From the Plan View, take the measurements from Point A to Points 1-4.

Transfer these measurements from where the Center Line meets the

horizontal line to the right side on the Elevation View. Label these points.

Step 4. Draw lines from the Apex to these points. These lines are the Slant Heights.

Step 5. For the Stretch-out, draw a line the exact length of a square side and label

the ends A. From the Elevation View set dividers at the Apex & Points 1-4

on the Elevation View. Transfer this length to the Stretch-out by using

both A Points as an Apex. Swing arcs, and where they intersect is Point 1.

Step 6. Set dividers from 1 to 2 on the Plan View and swing arcs to each side of

Point 1 on the Stretch-out.

Step 7. From the Elevation View, set dividers at the Apex and Points 2-3 on the

Elevation View and transfer this measure to the Stretch-out again using

both A Points as an Apex. Where these arcs meet, is Point 2.

Step 8. Use the same methods from Steps 6 and 7 to form Points 3 and 4.

Step 9. Draw an arc to each side of Point A using 2 the measure of a square side.

Step 10. From the Elevation View, set the dividers at the Apex and Point $4 and

transfer to the Stretch-out using Points 4 as the Apex points. Where they

intersect with the last arcs, are the $ Points.

Step 11. Connect all points to form half of the fitting. Add for lap, crimp and bead,
