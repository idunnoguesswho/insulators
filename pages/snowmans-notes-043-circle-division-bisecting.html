Circle Division (Bisecting)

Important!!

Draw cross hairs then add circle.

Place dividers on quarter point set to just over

half a 1/4 point and scribe outside the circle

slightly. Repeat for next 1/4 point joining arcs for

a common apex. Repeat as required.

Bisecting by thirds:

After scribing circle keep radius setting. Scribe for

1/4 points both ways onto the circle.
