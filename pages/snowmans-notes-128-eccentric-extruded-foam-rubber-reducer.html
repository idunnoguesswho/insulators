Eccentric Extruded Foam

Rubber Reducer

1. Cut a piece equal to the large OC (Insulation

included) & the actual height + 2

working edges.

2. Divide into 4 equal parts & put a working edge

on the top & bottom.

3. Subtract the small OC from the large OC &

divide by 3.

4. Place 1/2 the dimension on the side of 4 of the

4 1/4 points.

5. Mark lines from the above dimensions to the

1/4 points they span.

6. Cut out and glue together
