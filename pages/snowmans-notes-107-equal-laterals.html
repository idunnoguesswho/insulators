Equal Laterals

Dims Required: OD of pipe (Ins. included)

Working Drawing

Draw the working drawing. Draw center lines, add

OD of pipe and scribe arcs on all ends.

Divide arcs equally and project lines into

drawing and connect.

Tee Portion

Cut a piece of metal equal to the OC + LAP and ÷

into same number of parts as the

working drawing and label.

Transfer dimensions from the working drawing to

the stretch out.

Body Portion

Cut a piece of metal equal to the OC + LAP ÷ into

same number of equal portions as the

tee portion. Number according to LAP

location.

Transfer points from body portion of the working

drawing. #5 goes on line #3, #6 on #2,

#7 on #1.

Take all dims from one side of the working

drawing.
