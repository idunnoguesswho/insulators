Step 1. Draw an elevation view and establish an Apex by drawing lines

from both outside diameter lines to a point. This point is the Apex.

Step 2. Draw a half-circle on top of the elevation, trisect and label.

Step 3. Using Point 1 as a temporary Apex, swing arcs from all the

numbered points of the semi-circle to points on the horizontal line

of the large diameter fitting. Label these Points A-G as shown.

Step 4. Draw lines from these points to the original Apex point.

Step 5. Establish Point G for the Stretch-out and swing arcs from Points F, E,

D, C, B, and A using the original Apex as your swing point.

Step 6. Set the dividers from 1 to 2 on the semi-circle and take this set to

Point G and mark a point on Line F. Continue this walk-out and step |

down one letter each time until you reach the Line A, then step up |

@ one line at a time until you reach the Line G.

Step 7. Measure the slant heights of each lettered line and transfer these |

oe measurements to the corresponding lettered lines.

Step 8. Connect all lettered and slant height points to form the fitting.
