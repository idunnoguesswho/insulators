Draw a horizontal line with a 90 degree vertical Center Line and where they intersect draw a half-circle using half the O.D.I. Trisect or bisect the semi-circle into equal parts and from the horizontal line draw 90 degree lines up through these trisected or bisected points of the semi-circle. Label these lines. Using the radius set at half the O.D.I., do one walk-up on Line 1 and three walk-ups on Line 7. Connect these points and label them as Points A and B as shown in Figure I. Where the line drawn from 1 to 7 intersects with the Center Line, label that Point C. Set dividers at Points A and C and do one walk-up on the Center Line from Point C to form Point D. Connect these points going from A to D then to B. Continue the vertical lines from the arc points to Lines A, D, and B.
