True height, Square dimensions, Round dimensions Draw a Plan View and letter as shown. Mark the true height on the right vertical line. Trisect the bottom right quarter circle into equal parts and label. Set dividers at Point A and Point I and swing an arc from the Base Line to the right vertical line. Do the same for Point 2. Points I and 4 intersect as do Points 2 and 3. Set dividers at Point S and Point 4 and transfer to the horizontal line going from bottom right Point A to the left. Label the points on the horizontal line as shown. Draw lines from the Apex (true height point) to all points on the horizontal line. These will be the slant heights. For the Stretch-out, draw a line the exact length of a square side and label both Points A. From the Plan View, set dividers at the Apex and Points I- 4 on the horizontal line. Transfer these points to the layout from both Points A to an intersecting point on the Layout to form Point I on the Layout. Using a second pair of dividers, take a set from I to 2 on the quarter circle of the Plan View and on the Layout draw arcs to both sides of Point 1. From the Plan View, set dividers at the Apex and Point 2-3 on the horizontal line and transfer them to the Layout from both A Points to intersect with last arcs drawn. Do the same to obtain Points 3 and 4 on the Layout. From Points A, draw arcs to either side using half the distance of a square side. From the Plan View set dividers at the Apex and the S-4 Point and from the 4 Points on the Layout draw intersecting arcs to the last arcs drawn. These points will be Points S on the Layout.
