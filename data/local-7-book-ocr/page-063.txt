Draw a vertical line equal to the height of the tank with insulation included. Carry this line down from the tank top to a point that equals the full diameter of the tank. This bottom point is the Apex. Set your dividers from the Apex to the tank top and swing an arc to form the tank top. Divide the diameter of the tank top by ten. Use this measurement to swing an intersecting arc to form the curve where the tank top and the side of the vessel meet. Divide the tank top into six or eight equal parts and label. Place a straight edge on the tank top head and allowing a gap of 1/8" on each side. Obtain a Heel measurement. Round up or down as necessary to match the equal number of gores required to surround the total circumference. Draw half the Heel measurement required for a gore at the outside edge of the tank to the right, and then draw a line from that point to the Apex as shown.
