True height Draw a plan view of the fitting. Trisect and label the small circle as shown. Trisect and label the bottom 1/4 circles of the oval. Draw a 90 degree angle and take the measurements for the slant heights. These measurements are taken from 5 to I, I to 6, 6 to 2, 2 to 7, 7 to 3, 3 to 8 and 8 to 4. Place all these measurements on the horizontal line. Measure from the Apex to points on the horizontal line and label as shown. Start the stretch-out by drawing a horizontal line equal to the distance from 8 to 8 on the Plan View. From the slant heights set dividers from the Apex to the 8-4 Point and transfer to the stretch-out by using Point 8 as an Apex and swing arcs that will intersect to acquire Point 4. From the Plan View, set dividers from 1 to 2 and on the stretch-out set the dividers on Point 4 and swing an arc to each side. Retum to the slant heights and set the dividers from the Apex to the 8-3 Point and on the stretch-out, using Point 8 as an Apex swing intersecting arcs to form Points 3 on the stretch-out. From the plan view, set dividers from 7 to 8 and swing arcs to the outside of both Points 8. From the slant heights, set your dividers at the 7-3 Point and transfer this measurement to the stretch-out using Point 3 as an Apex. This arc will intersect to form Point 7.
