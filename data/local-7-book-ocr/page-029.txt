Draw a half-circle on top of the elevation, trisect and label. Using Point 1 as a temporary Apex, swing arcs from all the numbered points of the semi-circle to points on the horizontal line of the large diameter fitting. Label these Points A-G as shown. Draw lines from these points to the original Apex point. Establish Point G for the Stretch-out and swing arcs from Points F, E, D, C, B, and A using the original Apex as your swing point. Set the dividers from I to 2 on the semi-circle and take this set to Point G and mark a point on Line F. Continue this walk-out and Step down one letter each time until you reach the Line A, then Step up one line at a time until you reach the Line G. Measure the slant heights of each lettered line and transfer these measurements to the corresponding lettered lines. Connect all lettered and slant height points to form the fitting.
