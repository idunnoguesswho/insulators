Divide the radius and the height into six equal parts and label. Join like-numbers to form curve of the tank top; I to I, 2 to 2, etc. Divide the tank top curve into six equal parts and extend lines to the Base Line, and label those points as shown. To find the central angle, divide the number of gores required into 360 degrees and then divide the central angle in half. Place this measurement on Line I as shown. Add a 1/2" bead and crimp allowance to the Base Line. For the Stretch-out, draw a horizontal line and use the division measurement from Step 3 to draw a Stretch-out. Number the lines. Take the measurements from the bead and crimp line to the central angle line and transfer to the corresponding numbered lines of the Stretch-out.
