Determining the Radius of a Curve Geometric Method: Step I. Step 2. Step 3. Step 4. e = 16 Place a piece of cardboard behind the pipe and draw a section of the curve. Mark any three points along the arc and label them A, B & C. Draw lines from A to B and from B to C. Bisect the A and B line by swinging arcs from both Points A and B. Do the same for the B & C line. Extend lines down through the inter-sectors to form an Apex. From this Apex point to the arc of the pipe will be the radius of the curve. fig-re 1 Geometric Method CLR: Center Line Radius Algebraic Method: Chord: A line between two points on an arc. Step I.
